About us

Ris Build Ltd offers high level building services and market competitive prices for our clients.

The company is registered in the Central Professional Register of Builders.

Ris Build Ltd has its own warehouse, light and heavy machinery, formwork and facade systems and a team of experienced technicians, specialized in complete engineering of buildings and facilities of I, II, III, IV, and V category, certified by leading companies for construction materials locally and internationally.

The main goal of the company is quality performance and attention to the detail.
